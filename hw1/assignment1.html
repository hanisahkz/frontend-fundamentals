The first list:

  • 1/4 part of butter
  • 150 gram of cooking cream
  • 300 gram of pasta noodle
  • 500 gram of chicken (boneless part)
  • Mixed of herbs and spices (cajun, rosemary, basil leaves, spice mixes)

The second list:

  1. Melt the butter
    1. Ensure that it's deforst first
    2. Pre-heat the pan for 10 minutes
  2. Boil pasta noodle

    • Once done, rinse noodle and leave it to dry.
  3. Cook chicken part and mix it with spices.
